# Welcome to the Rowan Amateur Radio Society

The **Rowan Amateur Radio Society (RARS)**, call sign **N4UH**, is a friendly group of amateur radio operators in Rowan County, North Carolina. Whether you're a seasoned ham or just getting started, we'd love to have you join us!

## Meetings

We meet on the **2nd Monday of each month** at **7:00 PM** at:

**Rowan County Rescue Squad**
1140 Julian Rd, Salisbury, NC 28146

Come say hello on the air or in person — visitors are always welcome!

## Club Repeaters

| Repeater | Frequency | Offset | Tone |
