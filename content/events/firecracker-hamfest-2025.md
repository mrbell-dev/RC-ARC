## Firecracker Hamfest 2025

Join us for the annual Firecracker Hamfest! This popular event brings together amateur radio operators from across the region for a day of buying, selling, trading, and socializing.

## Event Details

- **Date:** Saturday, July 4, 2025
- **Time:** 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM (Vendor setup begins at 6:00 AM)
- **Location:** TBD

## What to Expect

- **Vendor Tables** - Browse and buy new and used amateur radio equipment
- **Flea Market** - Find great deals on radios, antennas, accessories, and more
- **VE Testing** - License exams available (contact us for details)
- **Door Prizes** - Win great prizes throughout the day
- **Food & Refreshments** - Available on site

## Admission

- **General Admission:** $5
- **Vendor Tables:** $10 (includes admission)

## Talk-In

Talk-in will be on the N4UH repeater:
- **2m:** 145.410 MHz (-0.600, 136.5 PL)
