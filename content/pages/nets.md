A guide to amateur radio nets in and around Rowan County, NC. VHF/UHF nets listed are within approximately 30 miles of Salisbury. 10 meter and HF nets with broader reach are listed separately.

*Always verify frequencies and schedules before checking in. Nets may change without notice.*

## Tips for Checking Into a Net

- Listen first to understand the net format before checking in
- When the Net Control Station (NCS) calls for check-ins, give your call sign clearly
- Follow the NCS instructions -- some nets are directed (you speak when called on), others are open
- Have a pen and paper handy to note information passed during the net
- If you're new, mention it! Net control and other operators are happy to help
