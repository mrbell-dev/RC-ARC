## Meeting Schedule

The Rowan Amateur Radio Society meets on the **2nd Monday of each month** at **7:00 PM**.

## Location

**Rowan County Rescue Squad**
1140 Julian Rd
Salisbury, NC 28146

[Get Directions on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rowan+County+Rescue+Squad+1140+Julian+Rd+Salisbury+NC+28146)

## What to Expect

Our meetings typically include:

- Club business and announcements
- Technical presentations or demonstrations
- Discussion of upcoming events and activities
- Time for socializing and Q&A

Visitors are always welcome! Whether you're a licensed ham or just curious about amateur radio, come on by and say hello.
