## Listen Live

You can listen to the N4UH repeater live through Broadcastify. This feed covers the RARS repeater system in Rowan County, NC.

<iframe src="https://www.broadcastify.com/listen/feed/45210/embed" width="100%" height="200" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen></iframe>

## About the Feed

This Broadcastify feed streams audio from the N4UH amateur radio repeaters operated by the Rowan Amateur Radio Society:

- **2m Repeater:** 145.410 MHz, -0.600 offset, 136.5 PL
- **70cm Repeater:** 443.250 MHz, +5.000 offset, 136.5 PL

## Listen on Broadcastify

You can also listen directly on Broadcastify:

[Listen on Broadcastify](https://www.broadcastify.com/listen/feed/45210)

## Note

Audio is streamed live when there is activity on the repeater. During quiet periods, you may not hear any transmissions. Check in during scheduled nets or busy times for the most activity.
